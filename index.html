Kun Wang (王坤)

I am currently a final year Ph.D. candidate at the Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong (CityU). My Ph.D. supervisor is Prof. Zhenjiang Li. I also work closely with Prof. Zimu Zhou. Before that, I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering, from Xidian University (XDU), China, 2020.

My research interests primarily lie in designing and building efficient AI systems on mobile edge devices (e.g., NVIDIA Jetson or HUAWEI Atlas), including:
(1) Developing efficient Deep Neural Networks (DNNs) inference engine,
(2) Building efficient Heterogeneous Federated Learning (HFL) systems, and
(3) Designing and deploying efficient Large Language Models (LLMs).

Note: I plan to graduate in Aug. 2024 and I'm looking for the job opportunities of Researcher or Engineer around (edge) AI Infrastructure, especially related to the acceleration of inference or/and training of LLMs.

If you have suitable positions, please feel free to contact me.
