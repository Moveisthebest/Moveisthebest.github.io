Kun Wang

I am currently a final year Ph.D. candidate at the Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong (CityU). My Ph.D. supervisor is Prof. Zhenjiang Li. Additionally, I also work closely with Prof. Zimu Zhou. Before that, I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering, from Xidian University (XDU), China in 2020.

My research interests primarily lie in designing efficient edge AI systems, including efficient DNNs inference engine, Federated Learning (FL) systems and LLMs deployment solutions on mobile edge devices.
